Economy System

The economy system allows members to earn, spend, and manage virtual currency within your server. You can track balances, banks, and transfer money between users.

Commands

  • !currency – Show your current currency balance.
  • !currency bank – Show your bank balance.
  • !currency deposit <amount> – Deposit currency into your bank.
  • !currency withdraw <amount> – Withdraw currency from your bank.
  • !currency send @user <amount> – Send currency to another user.

Admin Commands

  • !currency set @user <amount> – Set a user’s currency amount.
  • !currency addcurrency @user <amount> – Add currency to a user’s account.
  • !currency takecurrency @user <amount> – Take currency from a user’s account.
  • !currency view @user – View another user’s balance.

Tips

Encourage members to participate in server activities to earn more currency. Use role permissions to restrict access to admin commands.

Notes

  • All currency transactions are logged for moderation purposes.
  • Bank deposits and withdrawals can have optional limits set by server admins.
  • Currency is purely virtual and for in-server use only.
